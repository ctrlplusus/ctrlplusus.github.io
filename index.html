About

Our name Ctrl+, symobilzes 'zoom in', as a way to focus on details. Have you noticed that pressing [Ctrl] [+] on your PC or laptop provides magnification? We've translate that literal meaning into our company's core philosophy. We pay attention to details, whether it be an employee, or a customer, or a software application.

  • 2001-2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Dating back to secondary academia, and long before founders decided to create Ctrl+, eventual founders used the time to gain experience and industry knowledge in both private and governemnt entities.

  • 2013

    A company is born

    Like minded people with similar interest decided to start a company with intent to provide expert engineering solutions to U.S. Department of Defense and select private companies, using only the best software practices.

  • 2013-2016

    Transition to full service

    Ctrl+ founders, used the time to gain knowledge into the business aspect of software consulting as it relates to defense customer, and performed work for hire consulting for private industry.

  • October 2016

    Defense contracting

    Ctrl+ partnered with prime defense contractor.
